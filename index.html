Brittany M. Lane

I am a UX/UI Design student at the University of Minnesota, College of Continuing and Professional Studies (CCAPS) in partnership with edX .

My UX Skills

Empathy

As someone curious about the world, I have a lot of empathy.
I appreciate understanding life from the perspective of the user.
I characterize myself as someone who actively listens, offers
emotional sensitivity and awareness. When I am designing for
someone, I am thinking about them from every lens they see the
world from and the nuances that will enhance the future prototype.

Communication

I believe having good direct communication skills is critical.
More importantly to be inclusive in my design process and final
product is essential. I am not someone who is a stranger to having
intense conversations. I welcome challenging conversations and in
having those talks opens up space for new truths and possibilities.
I prioritize open space for talks, this is how I get great data.

Revisit

The design process is continuously iterative and there will be
many times where certain processes, cycles, or reviews have to happen
again and again. I understand when I am working on a project and have
become invested in the design process. The ultimate desire is to create
something worthy and purposeful. Also, I accept failure more now than ever.
I agree that failing means I am more closer to knowing what will be successful.
